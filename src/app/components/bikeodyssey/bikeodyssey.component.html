During my internship at iFormatLogic, I revamped a motorcycle shop landing page template called "Bike Odyssey." This project showcases my enhanced version of the original template.

Objectives & Goals

My primary goal was to provide ample breathing space for all elements in the project, aligning with my philosophy of creating visually appealing User Interfaces. Additionally, I sought to enhance the overall aesthetic by incorporating contemporary images and techniques beyond the template's standards.

This involved simplifying and seamlessly integrating navigation within an enhanced hero section, introducing a dynamic Products section, and prominently featuring renowned brands in the Partners section.

Problems and Thought Process

During the development of this landing page, I faced significant challenges, mainly related to frequent revisions and feedback from my supervisor. In response, I implemented a series of solutions. This included refining section padding, simplifying the colored lines beneath section titles, optimizing the navigation bar for improved functionality, and modernizing the appearance of the cards.

Lessons Learned

This project offered valuable insights into using gradients to create cards with full-sized photos while ensuring legible text. By juxtaposing the text against the image's black gradient, I learned a useful technique that blends aesthetics and functionality, resulting in a visually stunning and highly effective design approach.
