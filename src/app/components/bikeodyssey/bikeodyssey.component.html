A landing page task assigned to me during my Internship at iFormatLogic, Bike Odyssey is my improvement of a given Motorcycle shop landing page template.

Objectives & Goals

First off, I wanted the elements to have enough breathing room with one another in order for this project to help convey who I am as a person who enjoys creating aesthetically pleasing User Interfaces.

Additionally, I wanted to include pictures and techniques that were more modern and attractive than those seen on the template such as making the navigation simpler and placing it inside of a hero section that is also improved, adding a Products section, and adding more known brands in the Partners section.

Problems and Thought Process

The biggest issues I ran through during the creation of this landing page were numerous edits from my supervisor. My solutions included adjusting section padding, shortening colored lines under the section titles, making a more functional navigation bar, and making cards look more modern.

Lessons Learned

This project taught me how to use gradients to make cards with full-sized photos while still making the text inside it readable by contrasting it with the image's black gradient; for me, that was a really useful technique, it's beautiful and effective.
