Work

During my tenure, I had the opportunity to work with my first client outside of the initial training period. This client's project source code utilized their own MVC framework, ran on PostgreSQL, and was hosted on Nginx.

To enhance the user experience, I implemented a series of UI improvements and introduced various backend functions. These additions are intricate logic calculations for different transactions made on the site. This experience allowed me to further develop my skills and contribute to the project's success.

Objectives & Goals

My primary objectives and goals were to contribute to the project's development to the best of my abilities. I aimed to enhance the frontend by implementing UI fundamentals that I have knowledge of at the time, ensuring a user-friendly and visually appealing interface,and to completing every task assigned to me, demonstrating my dedication to the project's success.

Problems and Thought Process

One of the major challenges I encountered was the time it took to grasp the intricacies of the previous developer's custom MVC framework design. This complexity significantly slowed down the process of implementing backend functions.

It required numerous revisions to ensure the quality and robustness of the backend code. Understanding and adapting to the existing architecture posed a considerable thought process, but through perseverance and careful review, we were able to enhance the system's overall quality and functionality.

Lessons Learned

I gained several valuable lessons during this experience, primarily related to PHP. Firstly, I deepened my understanding of PHP, especially in the context of handling dates, which proved to be a challenging aspect. Additionally, I honed my skills in working with loops in PHP, making my code more efficient and streamlined.

Another significant takeaway was mastering file handling in PHP, which is essential for various functionalities within the project. Moreover, I developed a clearer understanding of the Model-View-Controller (MVC) architectural pattern, which is vital for maintaining a well-structured and organized codebase.

These lessons have not only improved my proficiency in PHP but have also contributed to the project's overall success and quality.
